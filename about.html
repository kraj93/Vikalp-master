Vikalp Law society is an initiative by the Students of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, New Delhi, to provide basic education/legal awareness to the weaker sections of the society.


Co-founded in 2011 by two law graduates, Mr. Ravi Mishra and Ms. Neha Choudhary, Vikalp Law Society, a registered law society under Societies Registration Act of 1860, currently comprises of a small core team of 8 members and 20 volunteers, which focuses on teaching the kids in the Subhash Nagar slum (Behind Pacific mall) , J.J Colony, Dwarka ( Near Dwarka Metro Station) , Kirti Nagar slum  (right next to Kirti Nagar Metro station, on the blue line) and M.C.D School, M-Block, Subhash Nagar, as a part of our Slum Education Programme.


We undertake legal workshops in schools having earlier taken in Bharti Model school, Nawada and Sr Sec all girls school in Subhash Nagar, teaching students the concept of Right to Information, Fundamental rights/Duties, Legal Aid, Lok Adalat etc.


Apart from this, we organise Cleanliness drives and very soon, we plan to open up our legal wing which aims to give free legal advice to the needy.
