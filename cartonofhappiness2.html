The Carton of Happiness 2

Vikalp law society organized a donation drive from 27th October - 29th October 2014. A carton was kept near the canteen and was open to donation in the form of notebooks, textbooks, pencils, pens, sharpeners, erasers, crayons, credit etc.
The donations were distributed to the children in the slum areas where Vikalp is currently active. These include the slums of Subhash Nagar, Government School in Subash Nagar, JJ Colony Dwarka and Kirti Nagar.
