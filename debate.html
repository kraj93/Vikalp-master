ALL DELHI INTER COLLEGE DEBATE COMPETITION, 2014.

University school of law and legal studies, Guru gobind singh Indraprastha University, New Delhi in collaboration with Sufi leadership school for training mind and soul and Vikkalp Law Society, New Delhi organized an All Delhi Inter college debate competition in the campus of IP University on the 24th of January, 2014.
The motion of the debate was that the youth is not being trained to promote the nation to the top of its glory according to the rules set in the constitution. The speakers were given a speaking time of total 4 minutes.
The event was inaugurated by the Vice Chancellor of IP University, Prof Anup Beniwal and the dean of USLLS, Prof M. Afzal Wani. The event saw participation from 15 colleges from Delhi and NCR as well. Each team had one speaker speaking for the motion and one speaker speaking against it. Each speaker’s speech was followed by two interjections from the participants as well as the audience.
After the fierce competition, the team from Delhi Institute and rural development came first, followed by Amity Law School, Noida and then Maitreyi college, Delhi University. Two individual consolation prizes were given to participants from Vivekananda Institute of professional studies and Amity Law School, Delhi.
The event was organized by Vikkalp Law Society. There were 7 core team members who were the heads of 5 different responsibilities like Registration of the teams, hospitality etc. Each core team member had around 5 volunteers for assistance, who are the working volunteers of the organization.
The valedictory address was given by Dr Charu wali khanna and Advocate, Mr Ashok arora of the sufi leadership school for training mind and soul and the dean, Prof Wani.
