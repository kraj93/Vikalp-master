CARTON OF HAPPINESS ! :D

Vikkalp Law Society had organised a carton of happiness drive from the 15th of october, 2012 to the 19th of october 2012. A box, was kept near the canteen , aimed to draw donations in any form, books, pencils, pens, credit which was distributed among our kids in Subhash nagar and Dwarka.
The credit donated, a sum total of Rs 7,140 was used to buy resources used in our teaching sessions like a blackboards, notebooks, pencils and erasors. The account was maintained in a transaparent way by our treasurer, Ms Neha Choudhary.
The drive was a huge success and our kids benefited a lot from it.
