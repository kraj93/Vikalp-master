Background

Vikalp Law society is an effort by the Students of Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University, Delhi. It aims to spread basic education and legal awareness among the weaker sections of the society.

Established in 2011, Vikalp Law Society, currently comprises of a small team which focuses on teaching the underprivileged sections of the society in Subhash Nagar (Behind Pacific mall) and Sector 1, Dwarka.

Vikalp was co founded by Ravi Mishra and Neha Chowdhary, Final Year Law students of University School Of Law and Legal Studies, Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University and now, Vikalp has 7 core team members and 25 volunteers in its family.

Our Vision

We make a living by what we get, but we make a life by what we give

- Winston Churchill


  1. Present law as a saviour to protect rights ensuring justice and not as a fearful object as commonly understood.
  2. Spread legal awareness amongst the people especially the impoverished and weaker sections of the society.
  3. Work as a medium between all sections of the society and to promote social harmony and integrity.
  4. Work for capacity building of the needy people to participate and contribute in the process of overall national development.
