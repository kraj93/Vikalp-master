CLEANLINESS DRIVE IN CP BY VIKALP LAW SOCIETY.

Vikalp law society conducted its first every cleanliness drive in Connaught place on the 27th of September, 2014. Our team started collecting trash from Block A of CP and went on to complete the full circle, collecting the trash in huge bags and then unloading it in the dustbins.
While doing this, we also distributed pamphlets among people promoting the concept of cleanliness in the environment and its importance. We also encouraged the hawkers and shopkeepers to keep a dustbin near their shop so as to maintain the cleanliness.
The huge response garnered by the drive encouraged us to come back with its second part on the 8th October’ 2014.
